A New Level of Intelligence at Kajotte Studio!

Published: August 31, 2025 | Category: News

A revolution in home automation? Yes! At Kajotte Studio, we're proud to announce that after several days of intensive work, the Kajotte SmartLife program, a part of the Kajotte Studio Autostart System, has gained powerful new features! It's no longer just a simple tool for Windows—it's now the brain controlling your smart devices on your home network.

We've overcome numerous challenges and successfully integrated control over selected devices from the Tuya Smart Life platform. What does this mean in practice? Full control over lighting, power outlets, and other gadgets, directly from our system!

From a Simple Outlet to Full Control

Now, we can:

Turn power outlets on and off in any room, helping to save energy when no one is home.

Control LED lighting in any room, turning it on, off, and even changing the color with simple commands.

All of this is done directly from our proprietary program, without the need for external apps.

The path to this success was not easy. We encountered numerous obstacles, from problems with Python library installations and network communication errors to invisible characters in API codes. However, every challenge we faced was a valuable lesson in patience and precise debugging.

We are incredibly proud of this achievement. It's another step toward creating a fully integrated and intelligent home that works just the way you need it to.

What's next? We're planning further expansions that will allow us to control other types of devices and automate scenarios, such as turning on lights when motion is detected.

Thank you for following our progress!
