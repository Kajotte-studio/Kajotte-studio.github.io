Kajotte Studio Autostart System

System Autostart Kajotte Studio (SAKS) is an advanced ecosystem of programs that combines automation with the highest level of security.

* Full Control: SAKS operates in the background, monitoring key system events such as computer startup and shutdown, and keeps you informed in real-time.
* Security First: Access to the system is protected by a unique, physical USB key. Without it, the computer automatically shuts down, safeguarding your data.
* A Cohesive Whole: From an advanced calculator to a notification system, all SAKS programs work together to simplify and secure your daily work.
