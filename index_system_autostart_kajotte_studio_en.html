Kajotte Studio Autostart System

Kajotte Studio Autostart System (SAKS) is an innovative project that we have been developing with passion and precision since January 2023. It is a coherent ecosystem of programs written in Python, created to increase security and automate work on your computer. SAKS runs in the background, unnoticed, to provide full control over the device.

How Does It Work?

The core of the system is the Login program, which acts as the central manager. To access the full functionality of SAKS, a successful login is required. With this solution, you can be sure that only authorized persons have access to your system.

SAKS is an intelligent system. Programs running in the background monitor key events on your device. They automatically log information such as:
* Computer startup and shutdown
* Start and end of battery charging
* Successful login to the Login program

All these events are instantly logged and sent as notifications. The system automatically sends email messages, informing you of the most important events. In addition, key notifications can be displayed as Windows notifications, giving you full control over your device and keeping you up to date with its activity.

Security First

One of the most important elements of SAKS is a unique security system based on a physical key. Starting the computer without the dedicated USB key results in the device automatically shutting down and blocking access to the entire system.
This solution guarantees that your data and programs are safe, even in case of unauthorized access attempts.
SAKS is the future of automation and security.
It is not just software, but a well-thought-out architecture that works for you 24/7.

SAKS Login

This is the central and key program in the entire Kajotte Studio Autostart System ecosystem. It acts as the main guardian, without which no other SAKS program can be launched.

Main Functions and Security

The main task of Login is to unlock and run other programs, as well as to have full control over the entire system. It has an advanced BSAKS lock that protects against unauthorized access.

Login allows you to enable or disable selected functions of the entire system and individual programs, giving you full control over their operation. From its level, you can also manage the system, for example, lock it, reset it, or even shut down the device.
The program also provides direct access to the Python interpreter and the command line, which allows for advanced configuration and interaction with the system.

Reporting
SAKS Login is equipped with a very extensive SAKS Report, which meticulously monitors and logs all events. Thanks to this, you have a complete overview of the history of actions, from logging in to changes in configuration.

Notificator

This is an advanced system within SAKS, consisting of several programs whose main goal is to keep the user informed about key system events. It acts as a versatile tool for monitoring and securing the device.

Functions and Notifications
When the user logs in correctly to the Login program or logs out, Notificator automatically changes the Windows wallpaper, giving a visual signal about the system status change.
This program also plays an advanced role in battery control. In preview mode, you can constantly track the charging time or the decrease of each percentage, which provides valuable data for analysis and better energy management.

Security and Locks
Notificator is also a key security element of SAKS. Within just 5 seconds of removing the dedicated USB key from the device's port, the system will automatically shut down the computer and block access to all SAKS programs. This extremely fast reaction guarantees that your data remains safe even in case of a physical breach.
All Notificator settings are fully configurable from the Login program level. You can enable or disable individual functions there, adjusting the system to your individual needs.

SAKS Multi-level Security System

The SAKS system focuses on multi-level security, which ensures maximum safety and full control for the user. Programs in the ecosystem can only be launched through the central manager, the Login program.

SD SAKS - Guardian of Proper Operation
One of the key elements of the system is SD SAKS (Detection System), which constantly monitors the correctness of the entire ecosystem's operation and the correctness of the USB key. It is an invisible guardian that immediately reacts when any irregularities are detected, such as an attempt to interfere with the system or unauthorized use.

In case of improper operation or removal of the dedicated USB key, SD SAKS automatically shuts down the computer and completely blocks access to all programs, ensuring that your data remains safe even in the most critical situations.

Multi-level BSAKS Lock
The heart of the security system is BSAKS (Kajotte Studio Autostart System Lock), which consists of three stages. To gain access to full functionality, the user must overcome successive barriers:

* Stage 1: The first step is to unlock the Login MOD program through the Login program.
* Stage 2: After successfully unlocking Login MOD, you can run the Sysi program, which unlocks the second stage of BSAKS, giving access to most of the programs in the ecosystem.
* Stage 3: The last stage of security protects the most sensitive programs.

Special Security for Key Programs
Even after passing through the three stages of BSAKS, some programs remain blocked due to their strategic importance. Passaks, Bankier, and IFR are programs that require unlocking the Register to run. Interestingly, the Bankier and IFR programs themselves only need one password, which emphasizes that the key security is access to the Register, which is protected by two passwords.

SAKS Calendar:

Your Time Management Center

SAKS Calendar is an advanced program that goes beyond simple reminders. Since its inception at the end of 2023, it has undergone a significant evolution, and its modules (including Sysi Calendar) were thoroughly rebuilt in 2025 to provide unparalleled precision and integration.

What does SAKS Calendar offer?
* Smart Notifications: The program continuously calculates the number of days until the next event. This information is automatically sent to SAKS Login and placed in the SAKS Report, as well as in the email notifications you receive with every login and logout. You will never miss an important deadline.
* Multi-level View: The Calendar provides a monthly and yearly view in several versions, allowing for convenient planning and viewing of upcoming events.
* Advanced Calculations: Just enter any date, and the program will precisely calculate how many days are left until today or another date you choose. The results are automatically saved in the SAKS memory, which allows them to be quickly pasted into other programs, such as SAKS Notepad.

* Full Personalization: You can freely customize the appearance of the Calendar, changing colors and fonts to perfectly match your work style.

SAKS Calendar is not just a tool, but an intelligent assistant that works in the background to help you manage your time with the highest precision and convenience.
